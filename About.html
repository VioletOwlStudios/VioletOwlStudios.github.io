This is Violet Owl Studios

Founded by EnderGames in 2026, Violet Owl Studios currently a solo dev studio. Our mission statement is to simply make games that people can enjoy for years to come. We are devoted to keeping our games free of the horrible gaming practices that plague our current gaming eco system (i.e: Loot Boxes, Micro Transactions, In-Game Ads...)
